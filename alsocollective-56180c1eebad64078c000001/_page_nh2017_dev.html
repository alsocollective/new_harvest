Top Innovators come together in NYC for cellular agriculture conference

  • 1

    What is cellular Agriculture?

    Cellular agriculture is an emerging field where agriculture products are made from cell cultures.

  • 2

    Who is New Harvest

    NEW HARVEST is a non-profit research institute that is accelerating the revolution around animal products made without animals.

  • 3

    What should I expect?

    Experience Cellular Agriculture, will feature the movement’s leading companies and researchers, who will meet to discuss the latest advancements in this critical field.
